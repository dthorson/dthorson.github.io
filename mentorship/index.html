What This Is

A reliable outside eye

Some people need something different. Not a deep dive into the interior. Not intensive weekly sessions. What they need is a trusted advisor they can return to monthly — someone who will tell them the truth.

This is a mentorship relationship. We meet once a month. You bring whatever is most alive — a decision you're sitting with, a pattern you keep noticing, a direction you're trying to clarify. I bring undivided attention and an honest perspective, shaped by years of conversation with some of the most interesting minds on transformation, meaning, and how people actually change.

It is a lighter touch than coaching. Less about going into the interior, more about orientation. Less about the depths of what isn't moving, more about seeing clearly and staying true. Over time, the relationship itself becomes an asset — a reliable outside eye from someone who understands your context and can offer perspective without agenda.

Who This Is For

You might recognize yourself

The person already doing serious work

You are reasonably self-aware. You're already engaged in your own development — perhaps through practice, therapy, or simply years of paying close attention to yourself. What you want isn't to be led through a process. You want a peer conversation with someone who can see things you can't, and who will say them plainly.

The leader who needs honest counsel

You're surrounded by people who have reasons to manage their relationship with you. You have few, if any, trusted advisors who will tell you the truth without an agenda. This relationship offers that. I have no institutional incentive, no stake in your decisions. My only interest is your clarity.

The person at a recurring crossroads

You face significant decisions regularly — in your work, your relationships, your direction. You don't need someone to walk beside you daily. You need a fixed point you can return to, someone who knows your context and can help you see what matters when you're too close to see it yourself.

How It Works

Simple, by design

This structure is minimal on purpose. The value is not in complexity or frequency — it's in the quality of attention and the continuity of the relationship over time.

Once a month

A single session, 60–75 minutes. You bring whatever is most alive. We work with it. You leave with more clarity than you arrived with.

Your agenda

There is no curriculum. No exercises, no homework (unless you want them). This is your hour — for whatever is actually calling for attention in your life and work.

Continuity over time

The relationship develops its own intelligence. After a few months, I understand your patterns, your context, your history. That understanding makes the monthly conversation more valuable with each meeting.

The Investment

$300–500 per month, depending on context and fit. Six or twelve month commitment.

The range reflects that fit matters more than a fixed price. We'll find what's right in conversation. As with all my work, I make special affordances based on resonance — if there's a reason to talk, the investment shouldn't be what stops you.
